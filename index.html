Faqs

Frequently Ask Questions

  • Can I use TNFT as metadata storage for Metaplex and Candy Machine?

    Yes you can. Immediately there will be instructions and tools available for download. Join our discord where you will find a dedicated channel for Candy Machine under TUTS & TOOLS.

  • Where do I start?

    Get started by downloading the TNFTCoin Desktop Wallet. You can install on multiple devices, no login details are required.

  • Can I just mine TNFTCoin for hodling, selling, swapping or other crypto trading activity?

    Yes. You only need to download the TNFTCoin wallet so that you can get your own TNFTCoin address and off you go. You do not need any special GPU's, Asics or other specialist hardware. Mine with your Core I3, AMD or whatever you have.

  • Was there a pre-mine of TNFTCoin?

    Yes there was. A total of 15million TNFT was pre-mined in order to fund the project, give to creators to get them started, reward anyone who contributes to the project, freebies ann more. TNFT tokenomics is public information and available on our website, in our discord and on twitter.

  • What is the block reward for mining TNFT?

    The block reward is 50 TNFT which will halve every 4 years. Block time is 45 seconds and a maximum of 295million TNFT will ever be produced.

  • Do I need some TNFT in order to create and store my metadata?

    Yes. Uploading your metadata to the TNFT blockchain is done via a simple transaction and there is a fee to pay. It is the same as most cryptocurrencies with regards to transaction fees.

  • Is my metadata available immediately once I have uploaded it?

    Yes. Metadata is available immediately and from any TNFT node, end point or your own local wallet.

  • How can I, without 3rd party assistance and no extensive technical know how, ensure that my metadata is permanent?

    Installing the desktop wallet means that you are already supporting the TNFT blockchain. Once you have uploaded your metadata, just open your wallet and allow it to sync. Thats it. You now have just what you need to ensure that your metadata is always available.
